iNQUiSi is built for your classroom. With a simple, web-based platform, iNQUiSi provides a better way for instructors and students to interact in class.

You ask a question, your students can answer from any device, and you get real time data analytics and instant feedback. With no hardware to fuss about, you can focus on the things that matter – like engaging your students and making sure they're on track.

Features

Engage Learners

Give each of your students a chance to communicate. Without having to call on a single student, you can reach out to every single student and get their input. With iNQUiSi, it's easy to keep everyone involved and learning.

Analyze Data

Effortlessly collect data about your course. When you have every student response at your fingertips, it's simple to see what topics students are understanding or struggling with. If a student understands Topic A, do they also understand Topic B? We'll crunch the numbers for you, so you can run your classroom more efficiently.

No Hardware

Nobody wants another piece of hardware. As an instructor, you shouldn't have to fuss with setting up a base-station and registering remote ID codes. With iNQUiSi, getting started is as easy as clicking a button. Students can enroll easily, and you can focus on asking engaging questions.

Mobile Ready

Why bother dragging a computer to class when you have one in your pocket? With a mobile-first design, iNQUiSi was built with smartphones in mind. Answering questions should be as easy as pulling out your smartphone and logging in, so now it is.

BYOD

What if a student doesn't have a smartphone? With our cloud-based platform, it doesn't matter what device anyone uses. If it has a web browser, it works with iNQUiSi. That makes everyone's lives easier.

Pedagogical

Pedagogical Research has proven that Classroom Response Systems are good for everyone in the classroom. Students are more engaged and retain information better than traditional methods, and instructors can learn to ask more meaningful questions. See for yourself!
